What is a pixel grid?
A pixel grid is a virtual space unit on our website. Each grid represents an advertising spot you can purchase. After purchasing, you can upload an image and add a link for visitors to click.
How do I purchase a pixel grid?
The purchase steps are very simple:
1. On the pixel page, select the grids you want (you can select multiple continuous grids)
2. Upload the image you want to display
3. Add the link address for clicks
4. Confirm your purchase and complete the payment
Once purchased, your image will immediately appear in the selected location.
What is the price of each pixel grid?
Each pixel grid costs $1 USD (or equivalent in RMB). The price is fixed; the more grids you buy, the larger your display area.
Can I change the content of a purchased pixel grid?
To ensure the uniqueness and fairness of the pixel wall, **the content (image and link) of purchased pixel grids cannot be modified or deleted.** Please double-check before uploading your image and entering your link. We will permanently display your submitted content in the purchased location.
What types of images can I upload?
You can upload common image formats like JPG and PNG. However, please note:
- Image content must be legal and compliant, and must not contain illegal information such as pornography, violence, discrimination, infringement, or fraud.
- We recommend using clear images.
- The maximum file size for a single image is 5MB.
- All image content is uploaded and provided by users themselves, and the copyright belongs to the original author.
- This site does not assume legal responsibility for user-uploaded content. If you have any questions, please contact the administrator for assistance.
How long is the validity period after purchase?
Your purchased pixel grid is owned permanently and has no expiration date. As long as the website exists, your content will remain displayed.
How many pixel grids will be sold in total?
A total of **14,000 pixel grids** will be sold for this project, on a first-come, first-served basis. No more will be added once they are sold out. This is a digital art project, and each buyer's grid is a unique, permanently preserved display that cannot be duplicated.
If they sell out and are not updated, is this a scam?
No, it's not. This project is a limited pixel display art wall, with a total of 14,000 grids. Once sold out, no more will be added. The images and links uploaded by each purchaser will be permanently preserved on this page as part of the digital art.
What payment methods are supported?
We support various international payment methods provided by Stripe, including:
- Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, etc.)
- Apple Pay / Google Pay (supported in some regions)
- Local payment methods are supported in some countries (e.g., GrabPay, FPX, etc.)
Stripe is a leading global payment platform, secure and reliable.
Can I get a refund after purchase?
We apologize, but all pixel grid purchases are one-time and non-refundable. Since your image will be displayed immediately after payment and occupies limited resources, please carefully review your uploaded content before confirming.
